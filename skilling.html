Skilling Program

A Skilling Programme has been organized for the undergraduate students for providing them exposure and practical experience to the facilities available in the BIT AICTE IDEA LAB. The event focused on explaining the initiative taken by the institute under AICTE. Dr. Arun Arora encouraged students towards application of science and technology for enhanced “HANDS-ON” experience and learning. Dr MK Gupta highlighted that the program is not only a transformation in technical education but also an approach towards “AATM NIRBHAR BHARAT” Dr. Pawan Patnaik gave immense appreciation to the institute dignitaries, the Lab Gurus and all the faculty coordinators to take efforts for giving different dimensions of learning and experience to the participants.All the esteemed guests and faculty members gave their best wishes to all participants and promised any kind of support and guidance. The participants were trained on “Laser Cutting Machine, Vinyl Cutter and Sublimation Printing by Dr. Santosh Mishra (IDEA LAB GURU), “3D Printing and Scanning” by Dr. Anil Kumar (IDEA LAB GURU), “PCB Designing using EAGLE CAD and PCB Fabrication” by Prof. Suchitra Pandey (IDEA LAB GURU) and “IOT Sensors and its applications”, “Programming of various Development boards like ARDUINO UNO and ESP32” by Prof. Kauleshwar Prasad (IDEA LAB GURU). The participants converted their Ideas into working prototype and tried to incorporate the skilling concepts practically in their projects. The participants felt very enthusiastic to learn that the advanced facilities have been established in our institute which will provide practical learning experience in the field of smart and intelligent manufacturing.
